Teleport Enterprise Cloud is the **fastest and easiest way to get started**.  

Infrastructure and certificates are managed for you. All that's left is deploying agents to enroll your resources.

To get started, simply sign up for a 14-day free trial. We'll automatically provision and manage a fully featured Teleport cluster in a dedicated tenant with a unique `example.teleport.sh` domain.

**To deploy your cluster:**

1. Go to [goteleport.com/signup](https://goteleport.com/signup)
2. Follow the sign-up steps to launch your cluster

When setup is finished, you'll have a production-grade Teleport cluster configured with secure defaults, automatic updates, and built-in scalability.

The signup flow creates a local Teleport user for you with the `editor` and `access` roles. This user can perform administrative tasks in your cluster. Keep this user's credentials and recovery codes in a secure location—they are your fallback for accessing the cluster if your SSO provider is unavailable.

<Admonition type="tip" title="Recovery codes">
During signup, Teleport generates recovery codes for your account. Store them securely. If you lose access to your multi-factor authentication device, you will need these codes to regain access.
</Admonition>
