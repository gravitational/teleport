Teleport makes it easier for your organization to achieve compliance with
different frameworks, including SOC 2 and FedRAMP. You can fulfill the access
control requirements of your compliance framework by applying configuration
settings within Teleport.

Follow our guides to see how to use Teleport to achieve compliance:

- [FedRAMP](fedramp/index.mdx) — overview of how Teleport covers FedRAMP control requirements, plus FAQ
  - [FedRAMP Deployment guide](fedramp/deployment.mdx) — operator walkthrough (FIPS build, cluster config, audit logging and SIEM, agents) plus tradeoffs of FIPS mode
  - [FedRAMP Audit Guide](fedramp/evidence.mdx) — what to do when a 3PAO asks you to demonstrate a control during a live audit
  - [FedRAMP Rev 5 control mapping](fedramp/rev5.mdx) — NIST SP 800-53 Rev 5 control coverage
  - [FedRAMP 20x KSI mapping](fedramp/20x.mdx) — Key Security Indicator coverage
