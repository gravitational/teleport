---
title: General security recommendations
description: Provides a simplified checklist of security measures to consider to protect infrastructure resources and Teleport clusters.
---

If you host the Teleport cluster on your own network, review the following checklist for actions you can take to make 
your infrastructure and Teleport cluster more secure and less vulnerable to different attack vectors. You can alleviate
many of these security concerns by using Teleport Team or Teleport Cloud Enterprise.

- Use specific role combinations to assign users the least privilege.
- Consider separating people from roles to ensure checks and balance.
- Consider all system-level access carefully.
- Require multifactor authentication.
- Consider the use of IP address pinning.
- Consider the use of moderated sessions.
- Enroll devices and configure device trust.
